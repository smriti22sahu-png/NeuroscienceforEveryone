Why Do We Sleep?

Sleep is not wasted time. The brain is busy.

Sleep is one of the most important activities for the brain and body. During sleep, the brain helps process information, support memory, and recover from the day.

Scientists are still studying exactly how sleep works, but it is clear that sleep is essential for learning, attention, mood, and overall brain function.

Why should we care?

Good sleep supports better thinking, better memory, and better health. In other words, the brain is not being dramatic when it insists on sleep.

Sleep is one of the brain’s favorite weird habits.
